Welcome to the Fraud Detection System

Our platform helps healthcare organizations proactively identify, analyze, and prevent fraudulent claims. With powerful analytics, machine learning predictions, and intuitive visualizations, you can make data-driven decisions to safeguard your operations.

How It Works

  1. Upload Data: Import individual or bulk claims data securely.
  2. Analyze: Run AI-powered fraud detection algorithms.
  3. Understand Predictions: View SHAP explanations to see why a claim is flagged.
  4. Generate Reports: Export detailed fraud analysis reports for auditing.

Key Features

  • Single Claim Analysis: Instantly check the legitimacy of a single claim.
  • Provider-Level Insights: Analyze trends and anomalies across multiple claims.
  • Explainable AI: Understand model decisions using SHAP-based visualizations.
  • Interactive Dashboard: Real-time statistics and charts for fraud monitoring.
  • Report Generation: Download evidence-based reports in just a few clicks.

Why Choose This System?

  • Reduce financial losses by early fraud detection.
  • Gain transparency into model predictions.
  • Make data-driven decisions with ease.
  • Secure and compliant with healthcare data standards.
